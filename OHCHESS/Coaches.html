Coaches

This list is presented for the convenience of parents or schools looking for a coach or tutor for their chess students. This list is prepared from information submitted by the coaches that are members of the Ohio Chess Association. The appearance of a coach on this list does not represent an endorsement by the webmaster or any organization associated with this web site.

We encourage you to check references before hiring any coach.

To be listed as a coach on this site you must be a current member of the OCA. Please send an email to:OHCHESS1@GMAIL.COM

Carl Boor (Central Ohio) Ohio Chess Academy is lead by Senior Master Carl Boor (3x Ohio Champion). Since 1996 our program has offered programs in the schools, private instruction (at home or online) and summer chess camps. All coaching is done by a native Ohioan chess master with 30+ years of tournament experience. Please visit www.OhioChessAcademy.com

Mika Brattain (Central Ohio) I am a Senior Master currently rated 2500 USCF.  I am very experienced with both scholastic and open competition. I know what it takes to succeed at either level, and I am looking to help motivated and active tournament players of Class C or above fulfill their goals. I am a very well-rounded player and can offer assistance in improving opening repertoire, calculation, intuitive thinking, endgame technique, and miscellaneous skills that will give you an edge during competition! Please contact me at mk.brattain@gmail.com or 339-223-1971 with any inquiries!

WHO WE ARE

The purpose of our association is to foster the study and pursuit of the game of chess by conducting tournaments for players, sponsoring teams to represent the state of Ohio in interstate and international play, promote chess organizations in the state of Ohio, and the overall general promotion of chess.
